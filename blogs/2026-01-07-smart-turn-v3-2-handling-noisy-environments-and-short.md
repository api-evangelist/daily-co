We’re happy to kick off the New Year with a new Smart Turn release, with two key improvements to the responsiveness of your AI voice agents.Smart Turn is an open-source turn detection model, which listens to raw audio data and determines when a user has finished speaking. Using Smart Turn, an AI voice agent can tell precisely when to respond to the user, without interrupting them, or waiting unnecessarily.As usual, all parts of the model are open: the weights, the datasets, and the training code.What's new in v3.
